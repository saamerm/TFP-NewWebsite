About

BGRS is a global workplace mobility firm that specializes in relocating employees across the globe. Headquartered in Burr Ridge, IL, USA, they are the leader in their industry and have offices across the world.

1. Case study

The company needed maintenance, feature development and republish of it's fleet of 6 apps in 2019. They were looking for professionals with the expertise in the entire Software Delivery Lifecycle of apps. We shifted the companies to start using better development practices, even using UI designs for mobile development, and created beautiful UI based on Figma mockups, using Zeplin integrations for iOS & Android

2. Result

With the help of TFP, the company was able to achieve all the milestones and deadlines. We also designed the implementation of Analytics to drive business decisions for investments, and the client has seen a lot of value through it

3. Experience

We led the development of iOS & Android apps used by the Canadian Armed Forces to enable relocation of their military members. In order to keep the 5 year old code up to date, we migrated the massive apps to the latest technologies - AndroidX, from MVVMCross 5 to 7.1, from PCL to .NetStandard 2.0, crushing nearly 50k build errors in the process.
